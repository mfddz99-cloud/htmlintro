Executive Summary

This report summarizes 2025–2026 performance and projections for major green energy sources, focusing on efficiency and short-term growth.

Purpose

Provide a concise technical overview for stakeholders and policy makers to inform investment and planning decisions.

Annual Growth

2025 Highlights

Solar and wind continued to expand capacity rapidly in 2025, while geothermal showed modest growth but high efficiency.

Resource Types

Solar

Rapid deployment and cost declines drive high percentage growth despite lower per-unit efficiency.

Wind

Strong efficiency and stable growth make wind a reliable contributor to the grid mix.

Geothermal

Very high efficiency with limited expansion opportunities compared with solar and wind.

Key Takeaways

  • Geothermal offers the highest efficiency but slower near-term growth.
  • Solar shows the fastest growth, driven by rapid deployment.
  • Wind provides a balanced combination of efficiency and steady growth.
